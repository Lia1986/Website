Incredible Returns
Without The Drawdowns

When we take standard trend following algorithms which have made billions in traditional markets and apply them to crypto they perform 3x better. We have no fundamental opinions about the merits of any individual coin. All trading decisions are made algorithmically, eliminating human error. We take positions in every crypto coin which starts to rise in value. And simultaneously take short positions in every crypto coin which starts to fall. By simultaneously trading over 100 coins in a long-short portfolio we are insulated from broader market trends while providing extraordinary returns.
