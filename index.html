The basis of FinRev is a strategy called Trend Following (aka Trend). Trend has been around for centuries and has been used to create massive fortunes in “traditional” markets by investing titans like George Soros, Paul Tudor Jones, and others you’ve likely heard of. Trend following is the idea that if something is going up, we should buy it because statistically it’s more likely to keep going up.

And if something is going down, we should short it because statistically it’s more likely to keep going down. With trend, you’re making a lot of small bets that things will keep moving in the direction they are going, up or down.
